Contact Quantorv Games

Last updated: January 15, 2026


Welcome to the official Contact Page for Quantorv Games. This page explains how users can safely and correctly reach us, what our official email address is, and how to avoid phishing or impersonation attempts.

Table of Contents

1. How to Contact Quantorv Games

At this time, Quantorv Games provides contact support exclusively through email. We do not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to get in touch.

The only official and valid email address currently used by Quantorv Games is:

  • Email: quantorvgames@gmail.com

This email may be used to contact us regarding gameplay issues, content questions, privacy concerns, copyright notices, or general inquiries related to the platform.

2. Important Notice About Email Authenticity

To protect our users from scams and impersonation attempts, Quantorv Games operates using one verified email address only. Any communication claiming to represent Quantorv Games from a different address should be treated with caution.

For clarity:

  • quantorvgames@gmail.com is the original and official contact email
  • Emails such as quantorv-games@gmail.com or similar variations are not operated by Quantorv Games

4. Future Professional Email Address

A professional email address using the Quantorv Games domain is currently in development. Until an official announcement is made, all legitimate communication will continue to come from quantorvgames@gmail.com.

If our contact email changes in the future, the update will be communicated transparently through official channels.

5. Staying Informed About Official Updates

To ensure you receive accurate and verified updates regarding contact information, platform changes, or announcements, users are encouraged to subscribe to the Quantorv Games newsletter.

Newsletter subscribers will receive official updates directly in their inbox, reducing the risk of misinformation or phishing attempts.

Quantorv Games does not currently use a contact form. Email communication is preferred to keep interactions simple, transparent, and secure.

7. Final Notes

We take user trust seriously and appreciate reports of suspicious activity or impersonation attempts. If you believe someone is falsely representing Quantorv Games, please notify us using the official email listed above.
