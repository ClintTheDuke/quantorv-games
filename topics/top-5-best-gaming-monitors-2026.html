1. MSI MPG 321URX QD-OLED – Best Overall Gaming Monitor

The MSI MPG 321URX QD-OLED is easily one of the most impressive gaming monitors we’ve tested in 2026. It combines a gorgeous 4K resolution with an ultra-smooth 240Hz refresh rate, making it perfect for gamers who want premium visuals without sacrificing performance.

The QD-OLED panel provides exceptional contrast, near-perfect blacks, and outstanding HDR brightness. Games feel more immersive, colors pop naturally, and motion clarity remains excellent even during fast-paced gameplay.

Another major advantage is its HDMI 2.1 support, making it a fantastic option for modern gaming consoles and high-end PCs alike.

2. ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27UCDM – Best Premium Gaming Monitor

The best gaming monitor we’ve tested overall is the ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27UCDM. This premium 27-inch 4K monitor features a stunning 240Hz QD-OLED panel with outstanding gaming performance and incredible image quality.

Everything about this monitor screams “high-end.” Motion handling is ridiculously smooth, HDR content looks phenomenal, and input lag is incredibly low. Competitive gamers will appreciate the responsiveness, while story-driven gamers will love the cinematic OLED visuals.

ASUS also packed in advanced burn-in protection technologies, which helps reduce long-term OLED concerns. That means we can enjoy beautiful OLED visuals without constantly panicking every time a HUD stays on screen for more than three seconds.
