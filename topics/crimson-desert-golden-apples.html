How to Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert

Hey Greymanes! Yes, you—the brave wanderer still trying to figure out why a hedgehog is apparently the most profitable creature in Crimson Desert. Today, we’re diving deep into one of the most searched secrets in the game: How to Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert, and more importantly, how to farm them without losing your sanity (or your bounty meter).

What Are Golden Apples in Crimson Desert?

Before we jump into How to Farm Golden Apples in Crimson Desert, let’s understand what they are. Golden Apples are rare loot items carried by special hedgehogs in the wild. They are not just shiny collectibles—they are crafting ingredients used for high-value Gold Bars.

If you’ve been asking “Where Can I Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert?”, the short answer is: Grey Rock Dock. The long answer? Keep reading, because it involves sneaky hedgehogs, timing tricks, and a bit of patience.

Location: Grey Rock Dock

To begin your journey on How to Obtain Golden Apples in Crimson Desert, travel northwest of Demeniss along the river line until you reach Grey Rock Dock. This peaceful-looking area is actually a hotspot for rare animal spawns—especially our apple-carrying hedgehogs.

Most players overlook this zone because it looks like a normal riverside dock. But trust me, it’s basically a hedgehog lottery machine if you know when to show up.

Best Time to Farm Golden Apples

Timing matters more than luck here. The best window for Crimson Desert Golden Apples farming is between 4 p.m. and 7 p.m. in-game time.

During this period, hedgehog spawn rates increase significantly. It’s like they all clock in for a part-time apple delivery job after lunch break.

If you arrive outside this time window, you might just find empty roads and question your entire gaming career (don’t worry, we’ve all been there).

How to Trigger Hedgehog Spawns

Now for the real trick behind How to Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert. Head to the rock corner near Grey Rock Dock and start running back and forth along the area.

Yes, it sounds silly. Yes, it works. You’re basically “refreshing” the animal spawn system by moving across the zone repeatedly.

Hedgehogs usually appear outside the nearby house or along the road. If nothing shows up, don’t panic—just keep moving past the area to force spawn cycling. Think of it like shaking a vending machine until it gives you snacks.

How to Identify Golden Apple Hedgehogs

Not every hedgehog is your jackpot. You are specifically looking for one carrying a glowing golden apple on its back. This is your signal that you’ve found the rare spawn tied to Golden Apples in Crimson Desert.

Once you spot it, prepare quickly—because these little guys are surprisingly fast for something shaped like a rolling spiky potato.

How to Capture and Collect the Apple

You have two main methods:

  • Shoot it using your bow for a clean takedown
  • Pick up the hedgehog and drop it to temporarily stun it

After the hedgehog is neutralized, the Golden Apple will drop to the ground. Now comes the important part of How to Obtain Golden Apples in Crimson Desert: stealth collection.

You must equip your mask before picking it up. Why? Because the area becomes a restricted zone once the item drops. Grab it quickly and exit the red circle immediately unless you want an unwanted bounty (and awkward explanations later).

Crafting Gold Bars from Golden Apples

Here’s where things get really profitable. At any witch’s shop, you can craft a Gold Bar using:

1 Golden Apple + 3 Gold Ore

This makes Crimson Desert Golden Apples one of the most valuable farming items in the game. It’s basically turning forest snacks into financial freedom.

Selling Gold Bars for Profit

Once crafted, take your Gold Bars to the bank and exchange each one for 500 silver. Not bad for something you picked off a hedgehog’s back, right?

If you repeat this cycle efficiently, How to Farm Golden Apples in Crimson Desert becomes one of the best early-to-mid game money-making strategies available.

  • Stick to the 4–7 p.m. spawn window

  • Don’t forget your mask (seriously, don’t be that player)

And most importantly, stay patient. RNG can be stubborn, but persistence wins. Eventually, you’ll master How to Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert and turn it into a reliable farming route.

So next time someone asks “Where Can I Find Golden Apples in Crimson Desert?”, you can confidently point them to Grey Rock Dock and watch them question why hedgehogs are now part of their financial portfolio.
