A Hat In Crimson Desert Let's You Make Infinite Money! Here's How

Wait… This Hat Is Basically Legal Cheating?

You know those moments in games where you find something so broken you just sit there like:

“Yeah… this definitely wasn’t supposed to happen.”

Well, welcome to Crimson Desert, where someone found a hat that basically turns you into a walking casino exploit 😏

We’re talking about the Deceiver’s Hat—an item that lets you literally see your opponent’s cards during gambling matches.

No cooldown. No penalty. No shame.

Just pure “I know what you’re holding, buddy” energy.

So… How Does This Even Work?

Let’s break this down in the simplest way possible:

  • You enter a gambling match
  • You press a button
  • You see your opponent’s cards

That’s it.

No complicated setup. No weird timing. No “only works on Tuesdays” nonsense.

It’s basically like showing up to a poker game with X-ray vision and pretending everything is normal.

Meanwhile, your opponent is sitting there thinking they have a chance.

They don’t.

How to Get the Deceiver’s Hat (Step-by-Step)

Alright, if you’re ready to become financially unstoppable in-game, here’s what you need to do:

  1. Go to Tommaso Yes, your journey to infinite wealth starts with a casual trip.
  2. Bring 20 copper pouches Think of this as your “investment phase.” Very professional.
  3. Give them to Seratien (Mineral Vendor) Because apparently… that’s how friendships—and broken items—are built.
  4. Reach 100 reputation Grind a bit. Smile politely. Pretend you’re not about to break the economy.
  5. Buy the Deceiver’s Hat for 97 silver 🔥 Honestly? That price is suspiciously low for something this ridiculous.

And boom—you now own what might be the most unfair advantage in the game.

Why This Is Completely Broken

Let’s talk about the real issue here.

Gambling in Crimson Desert isn’t just for fun—it can actually make you serious money.

We’re talking:

  • 30 silver per round (casual wins)
  • Up to 300 silver per round (big brain plays)

Now imagine having guaranteed wins.

That’s not gambling anymore.

That’s just… collecting income.

It’s like:

  • Playing chess while seeing your opponent’s next move
  • Taking a test with all the answers already highlighted
  • Or showing up to a race with a car while everyone else is jogging

Yeah. That level of unfair.

Meanwhile in Real Life…

This whole situation feels like:

  • Finding a “secret” discount code that gives you 100% off
  • Discovering your calculator app suddenly predicts exam questions
  • Your internet magically becoming fast only when you’re downloading something important

It’s amazing… but also slightly suspicious.

You Can Literally Become Rich Without Trying

Here’s the craziest part:

You don’t need to:

  • Steal anything
  • Grind endlessly
  • Fight high-level enemies

You just sit down, play cards, and win.

Over and over again.

Until suddenly you’re...Stacked with silver, Buying everything you want, and, Living your best virtual life!!

All thanks to one hat.

Honestly, this might be the most relaxed “get rich quick” method ever.

You should probably do this quick before the next Crimson Desert update rolls out, cause yeah, they might patch this.

Will This Get Patched? Probably 😅

Let’s be real for a second.

There’s no way this stays in the game forever like this.

Developers usually don’t look at something like:

“Players can see all opponent cards infinitely”

…and go:

“Yeah, that seems balanced.”

So if you’re planning to use this?

You might want to move fast.

Because once this gets patched, it’s going to go from:

“Unlimited money glitch”

to:

“Remember that one time we were rich?”

Final Thoughts: Fun Exploit or Game-Breaker?

If you're here, that just means your probably a big fan of the game. do you think this game could define the last decade of games?

There’s always that one item in a game that crosses the line from “useful” to “wait… this is illegal.”

This is that item.

It’s fun. It’s ridiculous. It’s completely unfair.

And honestly? That’s why people love it.

Quick warning: Don’t get too comfortable. If you build your entire fortune on this hat, a patch could humble you very quickly.

But until then?

Enjoy your villain arc.
