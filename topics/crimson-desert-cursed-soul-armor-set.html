Why the Cursed Soul Armor Set is Worth Hunting For

Hey fellow Crimson Desert explorers! If you’re looking to upgrade your survivability and look absolutely menacing while doing it, the Cursed Soul Armor Set is one of the best mid-to-late game armor sets you can chase. This full plate set not only offers solid defense but also carries that dark, intimidating aesthetic that makes every enemy think twice before charging at you.

I remember stumbling into my first piece by accident and immediately becoming obsessed with completing the full set. The combination of high armor value, unique bonuses, and the thrill of uncovering hidden locations makes this one of the most rewarding grinds in the game. In this detailed guide, I’ll walk you through exactly how to obtain every single piece of the Cursed Soul Armor Set. Let’s get you fully equipped!

Plate Gloves of the Cursed Soul

Our journey begins with the Plate Gloves of the Cursed Soul . Head to the Anvil Hill Logging Cave area in the Demeniss region. Once there, look for a glowing rock formation below the cliffside. This isn’t just any rock — channel a focused Force Palm directly into it to reveal a hidden entrance.

Step inside the newly opened passage and you’ll find a chest containing the gloves. The location is relatively accessible once you’re in Demeniss, but the glowing rock can be easy to miss if you’re not paying attention. Pro tip: Activate your mask or any detection skills to make hidden interactables glow brighter.

Plate Armor of the Cursed Soul

Next up is the centerpiece — the Plate Armor of the Cursed Soul . Travel to the cemetery area in the Demeniss region (clearly marked on your map). Search for an exceptionally tall gravestone that stands out from the rest.

Look closely for a leaf symbol on or near the gravestone. Perform a focused punch on this symbol to unlock a secret room beneath the ground. Climb down carefully and open the chest waiting for you inside. This piece gives a noticeable boost to your overall tankiness — perfect for those tough boss fights ahead.

Plate Cloak of the Cursed Soul

The Plate Cloak of the Cursed Soul is hidden near the Demenissian ruins. Keep an eye out for a tall tower in the area. On one of the walls, you’ll spot a suspicious square section that looks slightly different from the surrounding stonework.

Jump onto this square platform and deliver a well-timed force punch to break through into a hidden chamber. Inside, you’ll discover the cloak chest. The cloak not only adds to your defense but also enhances your overall silhouette with flowing dark fabric — great for style-conscious warriors.

Plate Boots of the Cursed Soul

For the Plate Boots of the Cursed Soul , make your way to Shadowwolf Cave in the Demeniss region. The entrance is hidden behind a waterfall. Use your stab ability to pass through the cascading water safely.

Once inside the cave, look for a suspicious wall and break it open with a force punch. The chest with the boots is located right after this hidden passage. These boots are excellent for improving mobility while maintaining heavy armor protection.

Plate Helm of the Cursed Soul

Finally, the Plate Helm of the Cursed Soul — the piece that completes your terrifying look. Head to the southern shoreline near the Steel Mountains, specifically around Windrift Cave.

Look for a smaller waterfall located halfway down the cliff line. Use your stab ability to enter through the falling water. Inside the cave system, the chest containing the helm awaits. This helm provides strong head protection and often comes with excellent set bonuses when the full armor is equipped.

Pro Tips for Collecting the Full Set Efficiently

Here are some hard-earned tips to make your hunt smoother:

  • Focus on the Demeniss region quests first — many unlock faster travel points and make navigation easier.
  • Level up your Force Palm and stab abilities before starting this hunt. They are essential for accessing most of these secret areas.
  • Bring plenty of healing items and stamina potions. Some caves have tough enemies guarding the paths.
  • Explore during daylight or use night vision if available — hidden symbols and glowing rocks are easier to spot.
  • After collecting all pieces, check your inventory for set bonuses. The Cursed Soul set often grants curse-related damage boosts or life-steal effects that shine in prolonged fights.

Collecting this set not only boosts your stats significantly but also opens up new playstyles. Tankier builds become much more viable, and the dark aesthetic pairs amazingly with cursed weapons you might find later in the game.

The sense of accomplishment when you finally equip the full Cursed Soul Armor Set is incredible. You’ll feel like a true force to be reckoned with across the deserts and mountains of Crimson Desert.

Now go out there, break some walls, punch some secret rocks, and claim the power of the Cursed Soul!
