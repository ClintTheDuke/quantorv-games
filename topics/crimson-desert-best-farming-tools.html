Best Tools in Crimson Desert for Farming More Resources

Why the Right Tools Make All the Difference

Hey Desert Wanderers! If you’ve spent any decent amount of time in Crimson Desert, you already know how important resources are. Whether you’re crafting powerful gear, upgrading your base, or preparing for tough expeditions, you’ll constantly need ore, timber, fish, and hides. But grinding the same nodes over and over gets old fast—especially when your returns feel painfully low.

That’s where specialized tools come in. The right pickaxe, axe, or fishing rod can dramatically boost your efficiency and drop rates. I learned this the hard way after hours of mediocre farming. Once I started hunting down the best tools, my inventory filled up faster and my crafting projects stopped stalling. In this guide, I’ll break down the absolute best tools for farming more resources, exactly how to get them, and pro tips to maximize their potential.

Mining Knuckledrill – Early Game Ore Monster

Let’s start with mining. The Mining Knuckledrill is one of the first major upgrades you should chase for consistent ore farming. Found in the Karin Quarry located in the Hernand region, this bad boy gives you a solid 10% chance to mine additional ore right away.

To obtain it, head to the quarry and clear out the enemies guarding the area. The tool has a decent drop rate from tougher mobs there. Once you have it, make it a habit to upgrade the Knuckledrill regularly. At refinement level 10, that bonus jumps all the way up to 30% extra ore — a massive increase that turns regular mining sessions into serious hauls.

I still remember the first time I used an upgraded Knuckledrill. Nodes that used to give 3-4 ores suddenly dropped 5-6. Over a full farming route, that adds up to hundreds of extra resources per hour. If you’re planning to craft lots of weapons or armor, this tool is non-negotiable.

Bernor’s Pickaxe – The Expert Miner’s Dream

When you’re ready for an even stronger mining option, go after Bernor’s Pickaxe . This expert-level tool offers a flat 20% bonus chance to mine additional ore, making it one of the best pure mining tools available.

Here’s how to get it: Travel to Fort Manub near Tashkalp in the Crimson Desert region. First, liberate the area from hostile forces. Then complete the “Lord of the Unclaimed Lands” side quest. Once the fort is under friendly control, Bernor appears as a camp vendor. Build trust by gifting him Copper Bags, then purchase the red book containing the recipe.

Crafting Requirements: 20 Fine Timber, 30 Iron Ore, 10 Sturdy Hide, and 10 Diamond.

Bernor’s Pickaxe shines in late-game farming routes where every percentage point matters. Pair it with your upgraded Knuckledrill for different scenarios — use the Knuckledrill for quick runs and Bernor’s for maximum yield on rare nodes.

Olvald’s Logging Axe & The Demenissian Chainsaw Upgrade

Timber is the backbone of almost every crafting recipe, so you need a great logging tool. Olvald’s Logging Axe delivers Expert Logging with a 20–30% chance for additional timber drops. It feels incredibly satisfying to chop down trees and watch the bonus wood pile up.

To get the recipe, travel to the Longbranch Shade Hideout in northern Pailune. Look for a large vine-covered tree. You’ll need a key to access the inner room where a red book holds the recipe.

Crafting Requirements: 20 Fine Timber, 30 Iron Ore, 10 Sturdy Hide, 15 Ivory, and 5 Rubber.

Once you have Olvald’s Axe, use it heavily — but keep an eye out for the Demenissian Chainsaw. Many players recommend switching to the Chainsaw later for even faster and more efficient logging, especially when clearing large forest areas.

The Claw Fishing Rod – Best in Class for Aquatic Resources

Don’t sleep on fishing! The Claw Fishing Rod is easily one of the best fishing tools in Crimson Desert, giving you an extra chance to reel in valuable fish with every cast.

Unlocking it involves some legwork. Complete different faction house quests in the Hernand region to gain access to the Vellua Fisherman’s Guild missions. After liberating the village, NPCs return to their spots. Head to the Bounty’s Net fishing spot and use your mask to stealthily steal the rod from the fisherman on the pier (or just fight him if you prefer the direct approach).

With the Claw Fishing Rod equipped, your fishing sessions become far more profitable. Rare fish and bonus catches make it worth the effort, especially if you need ingredients for cooking or alchemy.

Pro Tips for Maximum Resource Farming Efficiency

Having the best tools is only half the battle. Here are some practical tips I’ve picked up:

  • Always upgrade tools as soon as materials allow — the bonus percentages stack beautifully with your gathering perks.
  • Combine tool usage with region liberation. Liberated areas often have safer, richer nodes and friendlier vendors.
  • Focus on one resource type per session (mining day, logging day, fishing day) to make the most of your equipped tool.
  • Keep an eye on stamina management. Better tools won’t help if you run out of stamina mid-session.
  • Explore hidden camps and hideouts — many contain tool recipes or upgrade materials.
